About StudentGPT

StudentGPT was built to make learning feel lighter without making the work shallow. It balances speed, clarity, and practical help so students can move from confusion to execution faster.

Whether you are writing assignments, fixing broken code, preparing for viva questions, or trying to structure a final-year project, the goal is simple: give you something useful, understandable, and immediately actionable.

  • Focused on Students: Coursework, coding, projects, viva prep, and revision support in one flow.
  • Time Saver: Direct, compact answers when you are short on time and overloaded.
  • Learn While You Do: Explanations stay readable instead of sounding like textbook filler.
  • Practical Help: Useful for writing, debugging, planning, and turning ideas into next steps.
  • Always Available: Works when classes end and deadlines suddenly become real.
